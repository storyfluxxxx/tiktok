StoryFluxxxx – Short Video Automation Tool

StoryFluxxxx is a desktop tool that helps creators automate the production of short-form story videos. It combines AI-generated stories, text-to-speech voiceover, subtitles, and pre-recorded background gameplay into vertical videos optimized for platforms like TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and Instagram Reels.

The application runs locally on the user’s computer. It does not provide a public web service and does not collect personal information on external servers. When connected to TikTok via the official API, the user explicitly grants permission to publish videos to their own account.

All content, upload actions, and account permissions are fully controlled by the user. StoryFluxxxx simply automates repetitive steps in the content creation workflow.

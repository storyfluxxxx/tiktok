Privacy Policy for StoryFluxxxx

Last updated: 2025-12-02

1. Overview

StoryFluxxxx is a desktop application that helps users generate and upload short-form story videos. The tool is designed to run locally on the user’s own device and does not operate as an online service.

2. Data Processing

The application processes the following types of data locally on the user’s device:

  • AI-generated story text and subtitles
  • Audio files created from text-to-speech
  • Video files (background gameplay and rendered clips)
  • API tokens or credentials necessary to upload content to the user’s own social media accounts (for example TikTok or YouTube)

This data is stored only on the user’s device in the project folders configured by the user. StoryFluxxxx does not upload or sync this data to any external server operated by the developer of this tool.

3. Third-Party Services

When the user chooses to connect their TikTok or YouTube account, the application may communicate directly with the official APIs of these platforms in order to upload videos or manage content. In this case, the processing of data is governed by the respective platform’s own privacy policy and terms of service.

Any access tokens or credentials obtained via these APIs are stored locally on the user’s device and are only used by the application to perform the actions explicitly requested by the user.

4. No Analytics or Tracking

StoryFluxxxx does not include any built-in analytics, tracking pixels, advertising SDKs, or other mechanisms to monitor user behaviour. The developer of StoryFluxxxx does not receive telemetry or usage statistics from the application.

5. User Responsibility

Users are responsible for ensuring that the content they generate and upload complies with the terms, community guidelines, and privacy rules of TikTok, YouTube, and any other platforms they use.

6. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact the developer of StoryFluxxxx via the contact information provided in the relevant app store, repository, or distribution channel.
